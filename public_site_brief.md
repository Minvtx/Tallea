# Public Site Brief

## Purpose
Use this document to build or review Tallea's public-facing website and copy.
The website should sell confidence without overstating capability.

## Public Positioning
- **Headline direction:** Know your fit before you buy.
- **Core offer:** A reusable fit profile that helps shoppers choose likely sizes in participating apparel stores.
- **Merchant-facing promise:** Reduce size-related hesitation, support load, and avoidable exchanges.
- **Tone:** Calm, practical, precise, quietly ambitious.

## Allowed Claims
- Helps shoppers make more informed fit decisions.
- Provides likely size and confidence guidance.
- Works best with participating stores and clean product data.
- Uses body profile, fit preferences, garment data, and confidence modeling.
- Helps merchants understand where fit confusion happens.

## Forbidden Claims
- Perfect fit.
- Universal sizing.
- One-photo body certainty.
- True virtual try-on.
- Works across all brands automatically.
- Eliminates returns.
- Fully automated catalog understanding.
- AI that solves fit by itself.

## Copy Principles
- Prefer "likely fit" over "perfect fit."
- Prefer "confidence guidance" over "guaranteed recommendation."
- Make limits feel honest, not apologetic.
- Keep body-data language respectful and clear.
- Do not make merchants sound like the real customer on consumer-facing pages.

## Website Must Communicate
- Tallea starts with online apparel fit uncertainty.
- The first use case is practical shopping confidence.
- The product is useful today in participating stores.
- The company is serious about privacy and consent.
- The fit profile belongs emotionally to the user, even if merchants benefit.

## Website Should Not Communicate
- That Tallea already works everywhere.
- That the system understands every body and garment.
- That merchant conversion is more important than shopper trust.
- That body measurement is casual or consequence-free.
- That Tallea is only a returns-reduction widget.

## Suggested Page Sections
- Hero: shopper confidence promise
- How it works: profile, preferences, participating stores, confidence guidance
- For shoppers: less guessing, clearer fit expectation
- For merchants: fewer sizing questions, better fit intelligence, fewer avoidable exchanges
- Trust: data use, consent, limits, control
- Early access / pilot inquiry

## Runtime Use
If public events occur, update website tone cautiously.
Do not let press, pilots, or investor pressure push public copy beyond product truth.
