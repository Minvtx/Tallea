# Event Types

## Purpose
Use these event types as reusable runtime ingredients.
They are not a fixed plot order.

## Merchant Pilot Event
- **Trigger:** A merchant wants a closed pilot.
- **Pressure:** Revenue and proof arrive before the product is fully ready.
- **Likely empowered:** Nicolas, Lucia
- **Likely strained:** Matias, Rafaela, Camila
- **State effects:** merchant pipeline rises, catalog burden rises, product boundary tested.

## Catalog Mess Event
- **Trigger:** Merchant data arrives incomplete, inconsistent, or trapped in images, PDFs, or support memory.
- **Pressure:** Manual cleanup becomes unavoidable.
- **Likely empowered:** Rafaela, Matias
- **Likely strained:** Nicolas, Lucia
- **State effects:** manual cleanup burden rises, pilot timeline slips, automation story weakens.

## Trust Friction Event
- **Trigger:** Users drop off, complain, hesitate, or misunderstand body-profile consent.
- **Pressure:** Conversion and respect collide.
- **Likely empowered:** Camila
- **Likely strained:** Nicolas, Lucia
- **State effects:** profile completion changes, user trust position changes, relationship tension rises.

## Product Claim Event
- **Trigger:** Public language, demo language, or press interpretation implies broader capability than Tallea has.
- **Pressure:** Clarity becomes liability.
- **Likely empowered:** Lucia
- **Likely strained:** Matias, Camila
- **State effects:** public legitimacy rises or overexposes; pending reputational consequence may be created.

## Recommendation Failure Event
- **Trigger:** A high-confidence recommendation fails in a sensitive category.
- **Pressure:** Technical miss becomes emotional trust event.
- **Likely empowered:** Camila, Matias, Rafaela
- **Likely strained:** Lucia, Nicolas
- **State effects:** product confidence falls, trust risk rises, public claim liability may activate.

## White-Label Request Event
- **Trigger:** Merchant or platform asks Tallea to operate invisibly behind their brand.
- **Pressure:** Revenue and scale threaten user-facing identity.
- **Likely empowered:** Nicolas, Lucia
- **Likely strained:** Camila, Matias
- **State effects:** strategic identity shifts toward merchant_led or infrastructure_pulled.

## Platform Interest Event
- **Trigger:** Larger commerce actor asks whether Tallea can standardize fit data.
- **Pressure:** Infrastructure future arrives before user trust.
- **Likely empowered:** Lucia
- **Likely strained:** Matias, Camila, Rafaela
- **State effects:** public legitimacy rises, strategic clarity worsens, roadmap pressure rises.

## Runway Pressure Event
- **Trigger:** Cash, time, or fundraising pressure becomes harder to ignore.
- **Pressure:** Short-term revenue competes with product discipline.
- **Likely empowered:** Lucia, Nicolas
- **Likely strained:** Matias, Camila, Rafaela
- **State effects:** runway pressure rises, shortcut temptation rises, internal alignment worsens.

## Press / Newsletter Event
- **Trigger:** Local or regional startup media mentions Tallea.
- **Pressure:** Attention simplifies the company.
- **Likely empowered:** Lucia
- **Likely strained:** Matias, Camila
- **State effects:** ecosystem signal rises, public category interpretation may drift toward "AI sizing."

## Internal Repair Event
- **Trigger:** A conflict forces explicit acknowledgement of a buried tension.
- **Pressure:** Relationship truth interrupts productivity.
- **Likely empowered:** Depends on topic.
- **Likely strained:** Whole team.
- **State effects:** one relationship may repair; another tension may become explicit.
